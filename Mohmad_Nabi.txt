A torchbearer of cricket in Afghanistan, Mohammad Nabi’s repertoire as a complete cricketer - a confident utility hitter, a wily operator with the ball and a fearless fielder - is a unique asset that the Paltan can be excited about in IPL 2024.


Gully cricket at the age of 10 to initiating Afghanistan’s climb up the ladder to ODI status from 2008 to getting his nation to participate in three T20 World Cups (2010, 2012 and 2014) to scripting his country’s first-ever ODI World Cup win in 2015 - the many chapters of his fabled cricket career won’t be enough to fit this section.


The first-ever cricketer from Afghanistan to be picked at the IPL, he is now set to become the first-ever cricketer from his nation to don the MI jersey!
